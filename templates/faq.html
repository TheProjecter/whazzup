What's up — FAQ

Why are there [No title] feeds in my feed list?

If you've just added feeds, it's probably because the background queue hasn't gotten around to processing them yet.

If the feed is red it's because the feed couldn't be loaded. The reason could be that the URL is wrong, or something else. Click on the feed to see the error message.

If none of the above explain the problem, send me an email and I'll have a look.

How does the ranking actually work?

There is a bit of background in this blog posting. Also, if you want to know why a particular story has been rated the way it has, click the "[*]" under the headline in the story list. That will show the actual statistics, probabilities and overall score for the story. I've found this very helpful in learning what is going on "under the covers".
