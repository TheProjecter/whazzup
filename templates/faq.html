What's up — FAQ

Why are there [No title] feeds in my feed list?

If you've just added feeds, it's probably because the background queue hasn't gotten around to processing them yet.

If the feed is red it's because the feed couldn't be loaded. The reason could be that the URL is wrong, or something else. Click on the feed to see the error message.

If none of the above explain the problem, send me an email and I'll have a look.

How does the ranking actually work?

There is a bit of background in this blog posting. Also, if you want to know why a particular story has been rated the way it has, click the "[*]" under the headline in the story list. That will show the actual statistics, probabilities and overall score for the story. I've found this very helpful in learning what is going on "under the covers".

Why don't my votes take effect immediately?

The downloading of new stories and recalculation of scores all happen in a background process working from a message queue, and this queue can fill up at times. I'm working on making this part of the system more responsive.

What languages are supported?

At the moment only English and Norwegian. Other languages also work, but not as well. Languages written without spaces (like Japanese and Chinese) are unlikely to work very well, unfortunately. Contributions of support for more languages (stop word lists, tokenizers, and stemmers) are very welcome.

Where can I report bugs and request features?

Either by email to me or in the issue tracker.

Why isn't my question here?

Because nobody's asked it yet. Send an email to me, and I'll try to help.
